Multiplication tables check ICT survey

The purpose of this survey is to help us understand the information and communications technology (ICT) used in schools.

Please complete the survey using your school ICT. If you have a range of devices, complete the survey on them one at a time.

Following the connection test, please give us feedback on any ICT issues you encounter.
